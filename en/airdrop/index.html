NoBug Airdrop

Please connect your wallet before claiming the NoBug.

What is NoBug?

NoBug is a governance token of BurgerDAO. BurgerDAO is the owner of all smart contracts of project NeoBurger.

NoBug is designed as a sober governance token without explicit values for investment. That means the token is not regarded as an asset to be traded publicly and massively.

What’s the usage of NoBug?

    Raise a Proposal
    Vote
    Delegate

Participates can raise a proposal if hold enough NoBug.

A proposal is an execution of any smart contract on the mainnet. Proposals that receive more than 50% of approving votes before the voting deadline will be executed.

Contributors and Release Plan

Who is the contributor?

Everyone who continuously contributes to NeoBurger project for at least 256 days is able to claim its NoBug reward. (The list of contributors and their rewards will be public on github.)

Contributor release plan
